#MyCodingChallenges is a personal project that has the main purpose to share with the world my coding exercises and experiments.

For me, to see improvements on my coding skills and knowledge is one of the best feelings that exist, and the best way to get it is by practicing. Since I started studying web development - in September 2016 - it became an addiction and I basically don't stay a single day without writing or reading about it.

I got inspired by the #100DaysOfCode challenge on Twitter to finally put this project alive. My idea is also to post other personal and community challenges and exercises. Please contact if you have any suggestion or feedback. Thank you!
